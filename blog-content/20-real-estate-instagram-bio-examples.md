## How to Create Your Instagram Bio (Step-by-Step)

### Step 1: Choose Your Positioning
Pick ONE of these:
- Geographic niche (neighborhood, city, region)
- Buyer type (first-time, luxury, investor, relocator)
- Property type (waterfront, new construction, commercial, ranch)

### Step 2: Add Your Best Social Proof
Choose the most impressive metric:
- Sales volume (if over $10M)
- Number of transactions (if over 50)

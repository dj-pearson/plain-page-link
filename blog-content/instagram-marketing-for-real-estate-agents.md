## Frequently Asked Questions

### How many followers do I need to start generating leads on Instagram?

You can generate leads with as few as 500-1,000 targeted followers. Quality beats quantity‚Äî1,000 followers in your target market (local buyers/sellers) will generate more leads than 10,000 random followers. Focus on attracting the right audience rather than vanity metrics.

### How often should I post on Instagram as a real estate agent?

**Feed posts:** 3-5 times per week
**Stories:** 2-3 times per day
**Reels:** 2-3 times per week

Consistency matters more than frequency. Better to post 3x/week every week than 7x one week and 0x the next.

### Should I use my personal Instagram or create a separate business account?

Create a separate business account. This allows you to:
- Access Instagram Insights and analytics
- Add contact buttons
- Run ads
- Keep personal life separate from professional brand

You can cross-promote between accounts if desired.

### How long does it take to see results from Instagram marketing?

Most agents see first leads within 2-4 weeks of implementing an optimized bio + lead magnet strategy. Building a steady flow of 15-25 leads/month typically takes 60-90 days as the algorithm learns your content and you grow your following.

### Do I need to show my face on Instagram?

Yes. Real estate is a relationship business‚Äîpeople buy from people, not logos. Your profile photo should be a headshot, and you should appear in content regularly (Stories, Reels, behind-the-scenes posts). However, you don't need to be in EVERY post‚Äîmix personal content with educational graphics and property showcases.

### What's better for real estate: Instagram or Facebook?

**Instagram** for:
- Younger demographics (25-44)
- Visual storytelling
- Higher engagement rates
- Lifestyle marketing

**Facebook** for:
- Older demographics (45+)
- Community groups
- Long-form content
- Event promotion

**Best strategy:** Use both, but prioritize Instagram for lead generation and brand building.

### How much should I budget for Instagram ads?

Start with $300-600/month ($10-20/day). This is enough to test ad creative, find your audience, and generate 30-50 leads per month. Once you find winning ads, scale budget up to $1,000-2,000/month.

### Can I automate my Instagram posting?

Yes, use tools like Later, Planoly, or Buffer to schedule feed posts. However:
- Stories must be posted manually (or use Creator Studio)
- Reels should be posted natively for best algorithm performance
- DMs and comments must be responded to manually

Automation should handle scheduling, not engagement.
